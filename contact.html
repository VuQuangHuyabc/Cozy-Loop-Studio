Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We typically prepare orders within 1 day, and delivery takes 2-3 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Please see our Returns & Refunds page for detailed information.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes! We offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please contact us with your business information and estimated order quantities.

How do I care for my wool scarf?

We recommend hand washing in cold water with mild detergent, then laying flat to dry. Avoid machine washing and high heat to maintain the quality of your scarf.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we ship within the United States. For international inquiries, please contact us directly and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.
