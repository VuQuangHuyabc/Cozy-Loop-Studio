Our Story

Crafting warmth and comfort, one scarf at a time

About Cozy Loop Studio LLC

Cozy Loop Studio LLC is a registered business based in Santa Fe, New Mexico, with its official address at 1012 Marquez Place Ste. 106-B, Santa Fe, NM 87505, United States.

We specialize in crafting and offering premium wool scarves, focusing on comfort, warmth, and timeless style. Each piece is thoughtfully designed by combining traditional craftsmanship with modern aesthetics, delivering products that are both functional and elegant.

Our scarves are not only designed to keep you warm during colder seasons but also serve as versatile fashion accessories that complement a wide range of styles. We use high-quality wool materials to ensure softness, durability, and comfort for everyday wear.

With a commitment to long-lasting value, Cozy Loop Studio LLC strives to provide products that meet the highest standards of quality, comfort, and design in every detail.
